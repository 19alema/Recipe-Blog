classic cheesecake recipe

look no further for a ceremony and ultra smooth cheesecake recipe paired with a buttery graham cracker crust, no one can denny its simple decadence. For the best results, bake in water bath.

ingredients

graham cracker crust

1 and 1/2 cups(150g) graham cracker crumbs (about 10 full sheet graham crackers)

5 tablespoon (70g) unsalted butter, melted.

1/4 cup (50g) granulated sugar

cheesecake

four 8-ounce blocks(940g) full-fat cream cheese, softened to room temperature

1 cup (240g) granulated sugar

1 cup (240g) full-fat sour cream, at room temperature.

1 teaspoon pure vanilla extract

2 teaspoons fresh lemon juice (optional, but recommended)

3 large eggs at room temperature

topping suggestions, salted caramal, lemon curd, strawberry topping, chocaltte ganache, red wine chocaltte, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or raspberry sauce (recipe in notes)

instructions

1

Adjust the oven rack to the lower-middle position and preheat oven to 350℉(170℃)

2

Make the crust: Using a food processor, pulse the graham crackers into crumbs. Pour into a medium bowl and stir in sugar and melted butter util combined. (You can also pulse it all together in the food processor) Mixture will be sandy. Press firmly into the bottom and slightly up the sides of a 9-inch or 10-inch springform pan. No need to grease the pan first. i use the bottom of a measuring cup to pack the crust down slightly. Pre-bake for 8 minutes. Remove from the oven and place the hot pan on a large piece of aluminium foil. The foil will wrap around the pan for the water bath in step 4. Allow crust to slightly cool as you prepare the filing.

3

Make the filling: Using a handheld or stand mixer fitted with a paddle attachement , beat the cream-cheese and granulated sugar together on medium-high-speed in a large biwl until the mixture is smmoth and creamy, about 2 minutes. add the sour cream, vanilla extract, and lemon juice the beat, until fully comibined . On medium speed, add the eggs one at a time, beating after each addition until just blended .After the final egg is incorporated into the batter, stop mixing. To help prevent the cheescake fromdeflating and cracking as it cools, avoid over-mixing the batter as best as you can.

4

Prepare the simple water bath (see note) Boil a pot of water. you need 1 inch of water in your roasting pan for the water bath, so make sure you boil enough. I use an enetire kettle of hot water as the water is heating up. wrap the alumium foil around the springform pan. Pour the cheescake batter on top of the crust. Use a rubber spatula or spoon to smooth it into an even layer place the pan inside of a large roasting pan. Carefully pour the hot water inside of the pan and place in the oven. (Or you can place the roasting pan in the oven first, them pour the hot water in, Whichever is easier for you.)

5

Bake cheescake for 55-70 minutes or until the center is almost set. when its done, the center of the cheescake will slightly wobble if you gently shake the pan . Turn the oven off and open the oven door slightly. Let the cheescake sit in the oven in the water bath. Then cool cheescake completely at room temperature. Then refrigirate the cheescake for atleast 4 hours or overnight.

6

Use a knife to loosen the chiled cheescake form the rim of the springform pan, the remove the rim. Using a clean sharp kiniffe, cut into slices for serving . For neat slices, wipe the knife clean and dip into warm water between each slice.

7

Serve cheescake with desired toppings, cover and store leftover cheescake in the refrigirator. Cover and store leftover cheescake in the refrigirator for upto 5 days

yield
12 servings
cook time
1 hour
total time
7,75 hours
